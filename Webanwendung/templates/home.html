Welcome to AI Image License

At AII-License, we believe in fostering a community where creativity meets ethical artificial intelligence (AI) development. Our platform empowers users like you to create and embed licenses into the metadata of images, defining the terms of use specifically for AI training purposes.

About AII-License

The AII-License is a newly established licensing idea that aims to give copyright holders more autonomy over how their work is used for AI purposes. We offer eight different license options tailored to cover a wide range of applications.

License Picker

Our license picker allows you to create and embed licenses into your image(s). If you are unsure which license to choose, it will guide you through a series of questions to help you find the best fit for your needs.

Licensing Tool

If you have previously created a license and want to reuse it, you can use our licensing tool. Additionally, you can download our portable software to create and use licenses on your local machine.
