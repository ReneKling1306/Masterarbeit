What is the AI-Image-License?

The AI Image License is a newly established licensing idea designed to help rights holders have a say in whether their copyrighted works can be used to train AI models. The license gets embedded in the metadata of your images, giving you complete control over their use and eliminating the need to rely on others for protection. With our licenses, you can choose to allow, restrict or prohibit the use of your works for AI training.

What is uses am i allowing?

If you allow it, companies may create reproductions or derivative works of the Licensed Work only for the purpose of artificial intelligence training.

What are the license options?

You can choose from 8 different licenses which include up to three conditions that regulate the use of the images. These conditions can be combined according to the user's requirements. The conditions include:

BY

The "BY" (Attribution) licenses permit the use of the licensed material for artificial intelligence purposes of any kind, provided that the rights holder is publicly acknowledged for the use of his work.

NC

The "NC" (Non-Commercial) licenses permit the use of the licensed material for artificial intelligence purposes of any kind, provided that the intermediate or final products are not used for commercial purposes.

NG

The "NG" (Non-Generative) licenses permit the use of the licensed material for material for artificial intelligence purposes, provided that the intermediate or final that the intermediate or final products do not belong to the field of generative artificial intelligence.

There is also a Permitted For Training license, which is unconditional and allows the licensed works to be used freely for AI training.

If you wish to prohibit the use of your works for AI training, you can embed a Do Not Train notice instead. This will signal to companies that you do not want your work to be used for AI training without your consent.

How does the licensing work?

You can choose a suitable license using our license picker. The license will then be embedded in the metadata of your images. This allows web crawlers to recognise and comply with the license terms and conditions. The embedding process is carried out using Phil Harvey's ExifTool and will not alter any other data in your images except for the license metadata information.

Existing licenses can be overwritten by inserting a new AII license. It must be noted that previous uses are not affected by later changes. If you change a license, please note that you must revise all publications of the image. Outdated versions of your image with the previous license can still be used under these license conditions.

The licensing will come with a slight increase in filesize. If you plan to publish your licensed images to online platforms, be sure to check how they handle image metadata, as some remove it, and thus remove the license.

Metadata

The license information is stored in XMP metadata and may include the following tags:

aiContact aiLicense
LicensorName License
LicensorEmail LicenseURL
LicensorURL LicensingDate
UserComment LicenseCondition
