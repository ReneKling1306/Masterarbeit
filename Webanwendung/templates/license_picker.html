Choose your License

Are you familiar with the different Licenses or do you need help choosing the appropriate one?

Allow Use?

Do you want to allow the use of your image(s) for the training of AI-Models? If you are unsure what this means/entails you can click here for a detailed explanation.

Be attributed?

Do you want to be credited for the use of your image(s) for the training of AI-Models? If you are unsure what this means/entails you can click here for a detailed explanation.

Allow Commercial Use

Do you want to also allow the use of you image(s) for the training of AI-Models that possess commercial intentions? If you are unsure what this means/entails you can click here for a detailed explanation.

Allow Generative Use

Do you want to also allow the use of you image(s) for the training of generative AI-Models? If you are unsure what this means/entails you can click here for a detailed explanation.

Attribution Information

Do you wish to add attribution information to the License? If you have chosen a BY license you are required to add at least one form of attribution. Otherwise all entries are optional and serve solely to help interested parties to get in contact with you. Please make sure that all entries are correct!
